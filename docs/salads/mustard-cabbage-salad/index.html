Mustard Cabbage Salad

prep 10 minutes
× 6
This simple salad comes with a tangy, delightful dressing which is a perfect compliment to a savory dinner. pass

1

  • 5 tbsp
    olive oil
  • 2 tbsp
    lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp
    mustard, dijon
  • 1 tsp
    salt
  • 1 tsp
    pepper, black
Combine all ingredients in a sealed container and shake vigorously. Set aside to chill in the refrigerator.

2

  • 1 head
    cabbage, napa
  • 2
    carrots (shredded)
  • ½
    onion (in half-rings)
Reduce the cabbage to bite-sized pieces, discarding the hard core. Add the onion and carrots. Pour out dressing and toss before serving.
