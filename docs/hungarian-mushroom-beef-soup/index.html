Hungarian Mushroom Beef Soup

1 lbbeef stew meat
4 tbspbutter
1large onion (chopped)
1 lbbrown mushrooms (chopped)
2 tspdill weed (minced)
1 tsphungarian paprika
1 tbsptamari
2 cupsbeef broth
 salt & pepper
3 tbspflour
1 cupwhole milk
2 tsplemon juice
1 cupsour cream
1Heat the oil in a large soup pot and brown the beef in batches. Set aside to cool.10 min
2Add half the butter and saut√©e onions until soft and just starting to brown.3 min
3Add salt, pepper, dill, paprika, meat, and broth. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer.1 hour
