Jacket Potatoes

prep 5 minutes, cook 2 hours
× 4
These are the best baked potatoes ever. With a slightly different preparation, they wind up with extra crispy skins and extra fluffy, soft insides. I'll never go back to the "campfire" potatoes in foil again.

1

Pre-heat the oven to 400°F.

2

  • 4
    potatoes, large russet
Wash the potatoes, and then cut a quarter inch cross into the tops. Place in an oven at 400°F for 10min short of two hours.

3

Remove the potatoes from the oven and deepen the slits by another quarter of an inch. Return to the oven for 10 more minutes.

4

  • 4 tbsp
    butter
  • salt
  • pepper
Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly. Pinch to open up the potato and serve with a generous slab of butter and dusted with salt and pepper.
