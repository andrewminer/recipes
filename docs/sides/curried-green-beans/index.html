Curried Green Beans

prep 5 minutes, cook 10 minutes
× 4
I invented this recipe for green beans after spending a while playing around with Indian cooking. It has become one of my most-requested recipes. If you want to kick up the heat a bit, add a tiny touch of cayenne pepper.

1

  • 2 lb
    green beans
Steam the green beans until they are just al-dente. Transfer to a serving dish.

2

  • 6 tbsp
    butter, unsalte
  • 1 tbsp
    coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp
    cumin powder
  • 2 tbsp
    garam masala
Melt the butter in a large skillet. Once melted, add the spices and mix together until no clumps remain.

3

Pour the spice mixure over the green beans and toss.
